Why do I have to type the moving letters?

This is a tool that enables this website to protect itself from abuse and attacks by robots. By typing the moving letters that appears in the video you are proving that you are human.

How do I solve the Captcha?

Moving across the screen you will see a message. Trailing that message are some random letters. You need to type those random letters.

Don't worry about capitalization.

Can I just type the whole message?

Yes you can. If you prefer, simply type all the moving text.

How do I solve the Audio Captcha?

Check that your speakers are turned on and that your audio device is enabled.

Listen for the letters spoken in the audio sample and enter them in to the text box located in the Captcha Player.

You can listen to the sample again by selecting the 'Listen Again' link.
